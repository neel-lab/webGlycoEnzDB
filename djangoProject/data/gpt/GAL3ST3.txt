### Function

Human GAL3ST3 (galactose-3-O-sulfotransferase 3) is an enzyme involved in the sulfation of glycoproteins and glycolipids. This sulfation process is essential for the biosynthesis of sulfatides and other sulfated molecules, which play crucial roles in cell signaling, cell adhesion, and the development of the nervous system.
