Human CHST10 (Carbohydrate Sulfotransferase 10), also known as HNK-1 sulfotransferase, is an enzyme responsible for the sulfation of glycosaminoglycans (GAGs). This sulfation process is crucial for the function of various glycoproteins and glycolipids, playing significant roles in cell adhesion, signal transduction, and neuronal development.
