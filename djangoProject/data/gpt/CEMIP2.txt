CEMIP2 facilitates the depolymerization of high-molecular-weight hyaluronan into smaller fragments. This enzymatic activity is crucial for processes such as tissue remodeling, cell migration, and inflammation. By breaking down hyaluronan, CEMIP2 helps regulate the extracellular environment, making it vital for cellular dynamics and tissue homeostasis.
