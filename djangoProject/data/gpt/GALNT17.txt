GALNT17 catalyzes the transfer of N-acetylgalactosamine (GalNAc) from UDP-GalNAc to the serine or threonine residues on target proteins. This process is crucial for the modification of proteins, affecting their stability, function, and interactions, particularly in processes related to cellular communication and immune response.
