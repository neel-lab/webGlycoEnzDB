MGAT3 specifically catalyzes the addition of N-acetylglucosamine (GlcNAc) in a beta-1,4 linkage to mannose residues in the oligosaccharide chains of glycoproteins. However, alterations in glycan structures due to aberrant MGAT3 activity can potentially impact cell signaling pathways and immune responses.
