This modification is crucial for the generation of sulfatides and other sulfated molecules that are involved in cell-cell communication, cell adhesion, and other biological processes.

The presence of GAL3ST2 in the Golgi ensures that the glycoproteins and glycolipids are properly sulfated before they are transported to the cell surface or secreted outside the cell.
