Function
GCNT2 catalyzes the addition of N-acetylglucosamine (GlcNAc) in a beta-1,6 linkage to O-glycans, a type of carbohydrate chain attached to proteins. This enzymatic action is crucial for the formation of complex and branched glycan structures. These structures are key components of cell membranes and play vital roles in various cellular functions, including cell signaling, adhesion, and immune response.
