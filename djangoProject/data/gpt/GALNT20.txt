Human GALNT20, or polypeptide N-acetylgalactosaminyltransferase 20, is an enzyme involved in the initiation of O-linked glycosylation. GALNT20 catalyzes the transfer of N-acetylgalactosamine (GalNAc) from UDP-GalNAc to serine or threonine residues on target proteins. This initial glycosylation step is essential for the proper function, stability, and interaction of glycoproteins, which are critical in various biological processes, including cell signaling, adhesion, and protection of epithelial surfaces.
